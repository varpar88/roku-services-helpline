What is Roku?

Roku is a digital media player brand that makes it easy to stream content directly to your television. Whether it's Netflix, YouTube, Hulu, or live TV, Roku offers a user-friendly platform and hardware solutions to simplify your entertainment.

Top Features

  • Supports 4K, HD, and HDR streaming
  • Voice control with Alexa, Google Assistant, and Roku Voice
  • Private listening via the mobile app
  • Cross-platform search results
  • Wide app/channel availability (Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, etc.)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a subscription to use Roku?
A: No, Roku is free to use. You only pay for the content you subscribe to (e.g., Netflix).

Q: Can I watch live TV on Roku?
A: Yes! You can stream live TV with apps like Sling TV, YouTube TV, and more.

Q: Does Roku require an internet connection?
A: Yes, it requires a Wi-Fi or Ethernet connection to stream content.
